Comparison vs reference setup reaching 200 calls/day · drafted 2026-06-16

Context

A reference company doing a similar but distinct SDR setup reaches 200 calls/day at high lead quality. This document compares their stack to ours, identifies where the gaps actually are, and proposes a concrete 2-week roadmap to close the most leveraged gaps without scrapping our differentiators.

Their stack

  • Find companies: StoreLeads (e-commerce / webshop database) + Apollo for ICP-matching lists
  • Import to CRM: All companies dumped into HubSpot
  • Find the right person: Cursor integrated with HubSpot — AI flows defined per ICP, picks the relevant contact per company
  • Enrichment (phone): Full Enrich, called from inside the Cursor flow. ~80% direct-dial hit from a LinkedIn URL
  • Call: Aircall embedded inside HubSpot, call logging auto-syncs back
  • Tracking: Vibe-coded dashboard + HubSpot dashboard pulling from HubSpot + Aircall

Where the 200 calls/day actually comes from

The output gap comes from three things, in order of impact:

  1. Phone hit rate. Full Enrich hits 80% direct dials. That alone turns ~125 leads into ~100 callable. We hit ~50% so 125 → ~60. This is the single biggest gap.
  2. Source precision. StoreLeads gives "DK e-com store running Klaviyo" directly. We approximate this via "advertises on Meta in DK" — which is actually a stronger signal for active buying intent, but lower volume because not every fit-company is running ads right now.
  3. AI contact picking. Instead of dialing Apollo's first matching contact, they dial the right person picked by an LLM that read the company profile. Higher pickup-to-demo rate per call.
Key insight: Our "currently advertising" intent signal is genuinely stronger than their StoreLeads tech-stack signal. We're not behind on strategy — we're behind on enrichment tooling. Full Enrich is the highest-leverage single change.

What to copy vs leave alone

✓ Full Enrich for phone reveal

Replace Lusha and Apollo as primary direct-dial source.

Cost: ~$0.10-0.15 per enrichment × 30 leads/day × 22 days = ~$80-100/mo.

Net: roughly same total spend (Lusha drops, Full Enrich adds), with much higher hit rate.

✓ StoreLeads as an additional source

Subscribe and feed DK Shopify/Magento/WooCommerce stores into our Datafordeler-verify gate.

Cost: $250-500/mo subscription.

Net: bigger top-of-funnel without losing the DK-quality filter. Adds to Meta/LinkedIn — doesn't replace it.

✓ AI contact picker

When Apollo returns 5 contacts, use Claude/Gemini to rank them by "best fit for our cold-call script" given the company profile.

Cost: ~$0.002/lead — no new vendor, just a small LLM call.

Net: pickup-to-demo rate likely improves 20-40%.

✗ HubSpot

$50-150/seat/mo, locks us in, kills our custom-fit dashboard.

Twenty + our custom app already do everything we need. Their reason for HubSpot is Cursor integration — we don't need that.

✗ Aircall

$40-70/seat/mo. We already cancelled CloudTalk for the same reason.

tel:/sms: to iPhone works. The only loss is auto-call-logging, which could be added later via CallKit webhook on iPhone if needed.

✗ Cursor as orchestrator

They use Cursor as a no-code AI workflow tool — we have actual code. Cursor would add a layer of indirection without value.

Week 2
